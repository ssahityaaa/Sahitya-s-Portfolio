PROJECTS

Android App

I recently created a fun and interactive birthday wish app using Kotlin and Android Studio. This app allows the user to enter the name of the person they want to wish a happy birthday to, and then generates a cute and personalized animation with the person's name.

The animation includes colorful balloons, confetti, and a personalized birthday message. It's a fun and unique way to wish someone a happy birthday and make them feel special on their big day.

The app is user-friendly and easy to navigate. Users simply enter the name of the person they want to wish a happy birthday to and tap the "generate" button. The app does the rest, generating a personalized animation that can be shared via social media or messaging apps.

GAME-Stone Paper Scissor

I recently created a classic game of Stone Paper Scissors using Python programming. The game is simple yet entertaining, and allows the user to play against a computer by selecting a choice from stone, paper, or scissors.

The game generates a random result based on the user's choice and the computer's choice. The rules are simple: stone beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats stone. The user wins if their choice beats the computer's choice, and loses if the computer's choice beats their choice. If both choices are the same, the game ends in a tie.

The game is easy to understand and play, and is a great way to pass the time. It's a fun and challenging game that can be played anywhere, anytime.
